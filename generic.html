Recipe App

This app allows users to find and view the recipes that they are searching for, as well as input and store their
own recipes for their own or public use. Using firestore and firebase authentication it keeps track of user and recipe data,
and delivers it on reques. In addition, it allows users to select images from their camera roll to show the meal,
and stores them in firebase storage.
