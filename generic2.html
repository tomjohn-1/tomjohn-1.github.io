Weather App

An app that allows users to view the weather in their area and choose other areas to view as well.
The app makes calls to the API and returns hourly and daily forecasts, as well as extra information such as UV index
and pressure. The app also has a widget for the homescreen that allows users to see weather info
without visiting the app.
